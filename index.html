Objective of ICRIMSR

The objective of International Conference on Recent Innovations in Multidisciplinary Scientific Research (ICRIMSR) is to exchange innovative knowledge on Life science and Basic science among the scientific community worldwide through presenting their novel research as oral and poster presentations. This conference will provide a multidisciplinary forum for students, researchers, faculty members and industry peoples to present and discuss the innovations, challenges in life science and basic science.

Where

Karpagam Academy of Higher Education, Coimbatore

When

Thursday to Saturday
09th-11th June 2022

About the Conference

Welcome to the official website of the International Conference on Recent Innovations in Multidisciplinary Scientific Research (ICRIMSR- 2022).

About ICRIMSR

Despite the difficult moment that we are facing and fully committed to endure towards an insight on Recent Innovations in Multidisciplinary Scientific Research to stimulate the research on Biotechnology, Microbiology, Life Sciences, Chemistry (Physical, Organic and Inorganic), Physics (Medical Physics), Biochemistry, Technology Development and Applications and share research experiences among various institutions in all over the world.

ICRIMSR is being organized by School of Life Science and Basic Science departments of Karpagam Academy of Higher Education, Coimbatore to provide an opportunity to research scholars, delegates and students to interact and share their experience and knowledge in Life Science and Basic Science. (ICRIMSR -2022) will provide an excellent international forum for sharing knowledge and results in Life Science and Basic Science. The aim of the Conference is to offer a place to the students, researchers, faculty members and industry peoples from academic, research institutes and Life Science and Basic Science industries to meet and share innovative ideas in the respective fields.

Scopes and Benefits

In this conference, authors should submit their abstracts and full length research papers through on-line. The authors will submit their presentations in different formats: video (mp4), presentations slides or poster (pdf or ppt).

  • All the accepted research papers will be published in the conference proceedings and the selected full length manuscripts will be published in springer lecture notes "Science, Technology and Innovation Studies".
  • Awards will be providing for the best oral and poster presentation.
  • Each registered participant will receive the conference package (Proceedings, author/participation certificates and conference materials) by courier just after the conference.

Karpagam Academy of Higher Education, Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, India

Karpagam Academy of Higher Education (KAHE) established under Section 3 of UGC Act 1956 is approved by Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India. Dr. R. Vasanthakumar, the president of Karpagam Charity trust (KCT), philanthropist, industrialist, entrepreneur and culture promoter.

Contemporary infrastructure, modern teaching methodologies, career oriented training, excellent placements and the finest faculty have always become the Karpagam’s hallmark. Besides technical expertise, Karpagam Academy of Higher Education (KAHE) has made a mark since its inception by developing communication and soft skills, ensuring enlightening knowledge, extending holistic education and creating a strong value system. Today, with strength of 6500 students and over 750 teaching & non-teaching staff, Karpagam Academy of Higher Education is setting new benchmarks in the educational sphere.
